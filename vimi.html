Vision, Mission & Values

Vision

To be a premier center of secondary education in Dire Dawa and across the nation, recognized for nurturing highly competitive, innovative, and ethically grounded students who are fully prepared to meet the technological and academic challenges of higher education and global development.

Mission

To provide an inclusive, high-quality learning environment that equips students with robust scientific knowledge, critical thinking skills, and technological competence. We commit to supporting our professional educators, collaborating with parents, and utilizing comprehensive physical and digital learning tools to empower every student to achieve their maximum potential.

Values

At Sabian Secondary School, our daily operations and scholastic environment are guided by a firm commitment to key moral and operational values:

Academic Excellence

Striving for the highest scholastic standards in regional, national, and scientific benchmarks.

Integrity & Discipline

Fostering honest behavior, mutual respect, and high moral standards inside and outside the classroom.

Innovation & Technology

Encouraging modern scientific thought and resourcefulness through digital libraries and active experimentation.

Inclusivity & Unity

Celebrating the cultural diversity of our student body and promoting community growth together.
