Sabian Secondary School Background

Sabian Secondary School stands as one of the cornerstone educational institutions in the Dire Dawa chartered city administration. For decades, the school has been dedicated to cultivating academic excellence, fostering strong ethical values, and empowering generations of students to contribute meaningfully to society.

Serving a diverse and vibrant student body, Sabian has consistently adapted its educational frameworks to align with national standards while maintaining an environment that emphasizes scientific curiosity, community leadership, and social development.

1994 E.C

The year 1994 E.C. remains etched in our school's history as a transformative period of institutional rebirth and infrastructural expansion. During this pivotal year, Sabian underwent an intensive modernization process, expanding classroom capacities, upgrading laboratory learning spaces, and welcoming an influx of dedicated educators to meet the shifting demands of modern secondary education in the region.

Following the foundational upgrades established in 1994 E.C., the school continued to ascend, building a reputation for producing outstanding results in regional and national examinations. Today, the school seamlessly integrates traditional scholastic values with modern resources, including our emerging digital student libraries and community engagement portals.

As we look towards the future, Sabian Secondary School remains committed to its historical legacy—acting as a beacon of knowledge, diversity, and intellectual triumph right here in the heart of Dire Dawa.
